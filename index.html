About Me

My name is Ethan and I am years old. I am a Computing and Management student at Loughborough University and originally from Birmingham. Outside of programming some of my other interests include football, extreme sports and video games.

During my final year of school I still had no idea what I wanted to do next. I knew I enjoyed Computer Science but didn't know if I saw it as a career path, so once I finished school I decided to go travelling to give myself some time to think.

My brother was also in a similar situation as he had just graduated from uni. So with minimal plans we headed off to Dubai, New Zealand and Australia for 3 months from November 2018 - February 2019.

The trip gave the time I needed to think and while surrounded by beautiful views and the cleanest air in the world in New Zealand I came to the conclusion that I wanted to pursue a career in computer science; where most of my time would probably be spent in big cities in front of a computer screen.
