About Me

Hello! My name is Ethan and I am years old. I am a Computing and Management student at Loughborough University and grew up in Birmingham. Aside from programming some of my other interests include football, video games, extreme sports and fashion.

During my final year of school I still had no idea what I wanted to do with my life, let alone university. I knew I enjoyed Computer Science but didn't know if I saw it as a career path, so as a confused 18 year old I finished school and decided to go travelling.

My brother had also just finished uni and was in a similar situation where he didn't know what he wanted to do next. So we headed off to Dubai, New Zealand and Australia for 3 months from November 2018 - February 2019.

This gave us some time to think and while surrounded by beautiful views and the cleanest air in the world in New Zealand I came to the realisation that I wanted to pursue a career where most of my time would be spent in big cities in front of a computer screen.
